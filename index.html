Chapter 1: Meeting at the Coaching Center

Ek ladka tha jiska naam tha Vinay Kumar aur ek ladki thi jiska naam tha Shahnaj. Vinay Sant Kabir coaching me padhta tha, aur tabhi usi coaching me ek ladki padhne aayi jiska naam Shahnaj tha. Vinay Shahnaj ko like kar raha tha aur Shahnaj bhi Vinay ko thoda-thoda dekh rahi thi. Us samay Shahnaj bahut patli thi aur Vinay usko hamesha dekhta tha, usse baat karna chahta tha. Shahnaj ko bhi shayad is baat ka pata tha. Ek din, Shahnaj ne mela me Vinay ki behan ko apna number diya aur bola call karna. Us number se Vinay dheere-dheere baat karne laga. Shahnaj ki bari behan Tamanna ko pata tha ki Vinay baat kar raha hai, bas ek dost ke jaise, aur wo baat bhi karwa deti thi.

Chapter 2:Vinay aur Shahnaj ke Beech Mein Pehli Baat-Cheet

Dheere-dheere samay bitne laga aur ek din Vinay ne Shahnaj ko bola "I love you." Bahut darte-darte bola tha to Shahnaj ka bhi udhar se reply aa gaya, "I love you too." Fir dono me love wali batein start ho gayi. Lekin Tamanna ko ye baat pata nahi thi ki dono ab pyar me hain. Tamanna sochti thi ki Vinay bas ek dost ke jaisa baat karta hai. Ek din Tamanna ne chat padh liya aur usko pata chal gaya. Dheere-dheere Tamanna bhi bahut support karne lagi baat karne me.

Chapter 3:Pehli Mulaqat aur Vinay ka Dare-dare Tareeke se Hug

Sab kuchh theek chal raha tha. Jab Vinay 10th ka exam dene wala tha, tabhi thand ka mahina tha. Vinay ne milne ke liye Shahnaj ko bola aur plan kiya. Vinay gya milne usse. Us din Shahnaj jis dress me aayi thi, wo dress Vinay ko bahut achha laga. Shahnaj bahut sundar dikh rahi thi. Pehli mulaqat me Shahnaj 2 meter ki doori pe khadi thi. Vinay darte-darte uske paas gaya aur uska haath pakad ke usko apni taraf kheencha aur hug kar liya. Us samay Vinay ka pair bahut thar-thar raha tha, matlab kap raha tha. Vinay ka pehla time tha, aur Shahnaj ka bhi pehla time tha. Lekin wo apne pair se Vinay ke pair ko dab rahi thi taki Vinay ka pair na hile, lekin Vinay ka pair bahut tez se hil raha tha. Uske baad Vinay ne kiss karne ka try kiya. Shahnaj ke hoth bahut mast lage, jaise Vinay kha jayega uske hoth. Bahut achhe se kiss kiya Vinay ne.

Chapter 4: Romantic Relationship ka Vikas

Uske baad dono lagatar milte rahe aur baatein romance me badalne lagi. Vinay kareeb hone ka socha aur ek din sex se related plan kiya. Vinay ne Shahnaj se milne ka plan banaya aur Tamanna ko bhi pata tha ki Vinay aaj milega. Tamanna ne help bhi ki. Pehli baar sex me problem hui, lekin dheere-dheere sab kuchh normal ho gaya. Tamanna se chhupa kar Shahnaj Vinay se milti rahi aur sex dono ke liye common ho gaya. Dono ne shaadi tak ka plan kar liya tha.

Chapter 5:Tamanna ki Jaankari aur Support

Sab sahi chal raha tha. Jab 12th me admission hua to dono ek hi coaching me class join kiye. Dono sath me padhai karne lage. Tabhi Shahnaj ki ek dost thi Banti jisse Vinay dheere-dheere baat karne laga. Vinay ko laga ki Banti usko like karti hai to Vinay ne usko ek din propose kar diya. Banti ne accept kar liya. Vinay Shahnaj se chhupakar Banti se baat karne laga aur bahut din tak baat ki. Vinay ne Banti se bhi 5 bar mila, usko hug kiya aur kaafi kuchh kiya.

Chapter 6: Vinay ki Banti ke Sath Dosti aur Uska Propose

1Vinay Shahnaj se bahut pyar karta tha lekin usko Banti se attraction ho gaya tha. Dheere-dheere Shahnaj ko pata chal gaya. Banti ke ghar uske uncle ke bete ki shaadi thi, us shaadi me Shahnaj bhi gayi thi. Vinay ne Banti se chat kiya tha jo Shahnaj ne dekh liya. Banti pahle se hi kisi aur ladke se baat karti thi aur sex bhi kar chuki thi. Shahnaj jab Vinay ka chat dekhi to bahut tut gayi. Bahut royi aur Vinay ne usko bahut manaya. Shahnaj ne Vinay ko bola ki tum usko chhodo ya mujhe chhodo, kisi ek ko chuno. Vinay ne Shahnaj ko chuna. Shahnaj ne Vinay ko maaf kar diya lekin wo tut gayi thi. Dheere-dheere Vinay ke pyar ne Shahnaj ko pahle jaisa kar diya aur dono aur bhi gahre pyar me ho gaye.

Chapter 7: Shahnaj ke Saath Vinay ka Affair aur Uska Asar

Vinay aur Shahnaj ka 12th complete ho gaya. Shahnaj bhi padhti thi aur Vinay bhi padhta tha. Ek din Vinay ne B.Tech me admission le liya apne teacher ke help se. Usme Shahnaj ne bahut madad ki. Vinay Shahnaj ko apni wife banana chahta tha, isliye usne socha B.Tech karega to achha job milega aur paisa jyada hoga to Shahnaj ke family ko mana sakega. Jab Vinay Bihar se Chennai college me aane wala tha, to Shahnaj bahut akela feel kar rahi thi aur ro bhi rahi thi. Shahnaj ne Vinay ke liye jute, kapde, tie, sab kharid ke diye.

Chapter 8: Vinay aur Shahnaj ka Pyaar aur Uski Misaal

Vinay college chala gaya aur Shahnaj coaching padhati rahi. Jab bhi Vinay ko paisa ka madad chahiye hota tha, Shahnaj madad karti thi. Vinay nahi chahta tha ki Shahnaj kisi ladke ke najar me aaye, isliye Shahnaj ko social media se door rakhta tha. Jab Vinay college gaya to Shahnaj ne chori se Instagram id banayi aur chalane lagi. Vinay ko yeh baat pata chalne lagi aur jhagra hone laga. Shahnaj Delhi apne papa ke paas chali gayi.

Chapter 9: Vinay ki B.Tech Admission aur Shahnaj ka Support

Delhi me uske mummy-papa ek ladke ke bare me bole jo bachpan me shadi karne ke liye bola tha. Vinay dar gaya ki Shahnaj us ladke se baat na karne lage, lekin Shahnaj nahi maani aur Delhi chali gayi. Waha pe usne ek ladka dost bana liya. Shahnaj Vinay se kam baat karne lagi aur bahana dene lagi ki bhai hai, papa hai. Ek time aaya jab Shahnaj ek mahine me 2-3 bar hi baat karne lagi. Dheere-dheere Vinay aur Shahnaj ka 12th complete ho gaya.

Chapter 10: Social Media aur Shahnaj ka Delhi Shift

Vinay B.Tech kar raha tha aur Shahnaj school me padhane lagi. Dheere-dheere Vinay aur Shahnaj ke beech jhagre hone lage. Shahnaj Vinay ko block karne lagi. Vinay ko samajh nahi aa raha tha kya kare. Usne apne friends se paisa collect kiya aur Shahnaj se milne gaya. Vinay ne Shahnaj se baat karne ke liye Tamanna se request ki. Dheere-dheere Vinay aur Shahnaj ki baatein normal hone lagi.

Chapter 11: Vinay aur Shahnaj ke Beech Ka Jhagda aur Uska Samadhan

Vinay ke college me exam start hone wala tha, tabhi Shahnaj ne Vinay ko fir se block kar diya. Vinay ko bahut problem hone lagi. Usne fir se apne friends aur family se madad mangi aur Shahnaj se baat karne ki koshish ki. Dheere-dheere Shahnaj ne Vinay ko unblock kar diya aur baatein normal hone lagi. Vinay aur Shahnaj ka prem abhi bhi chal raha hai, lekin beech-mein jhagre aur misunderstandings bhi hote rehte hain.
